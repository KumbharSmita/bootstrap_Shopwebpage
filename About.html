About Us

Shree Dtta Krushiseva Kendra,Kundal

Chemical or organic materials used as fertilisers help crops thrive. One of the most widespread myths is thatfertilisers are solely used by farmers. Contrarily, fertilisers are essential for all kinds of trees and plants. However, farmers use greater amounts of pesticides and fertilisers to make sure that soil fertility or pests won't damage their output. Urban farmers, home gardeners, and gardeners all require frequent soil fertilisation.


Shree Datta Krushiseva Kendra is known for providing top quality fertilisers,composts, and insecticides.

MISSION


Our mission is to provide high-quality fertilizers and related products to support the agricultural community in maximizing their crop yields and promoting sustainable farming practices. We strive to be a trusted and reliable partner for farmers, offering a wide range of fertilizers and expert advice to help them achieve their goals.

VISION


Our vision is to become the leading fertilizer shop in our region, recognized for our exceptional products, personalized service, and commitment to environmental stewardship. We aim to contribute to the growth and prosperity of the agricultural sector by empowering farmers with innovative solutions and fostering long-term sustainability in farming practices.
